About AETHER

We are building the infrastructure that the next century will be built on.

Founded by physicists, compiler engineers, and aerospace architects, AETHER operates at the intersection of fundamental research and industrial-scale deployment. Our laboratories in Zürich, Pasadena, and Tsukuba develop the materials, control systems, and orchestration software required to make quantum coherence a continuous, available resource.

We do not believe the future arrives in announcements. It arrives in millimeters of cabling, in nanoseconds of timing, in decades of patient work. AETHER is that work, made visible.
